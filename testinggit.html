An avid food enthusiast who did nothing but critic food places and compare everything to his favorite food places!

Jollibee

As a self-proclaimed burger connoisseur, I can confidently say that Jollibee's burgers are a cut above the rest. The Yumburger, with its soft bun hugging a thick and juicy patty, topped with fresh lettuce, cheese, and a tangy signature sauce, creates an explosion of flavors in every bite. It's a harmonious symphony of taste that satisfies my burger cravings like no other.

Mang Inasal

I tried Mang Inasal during my visit to the Philippines, and it exceeded my expectations. The chicken was perfectly grilled with a smoky flavor, and the accompanying soy sauce and calamansi provided a tangy kick. The restaurant had a vibrant and lively atmosphere, and the prices were very reasonable. I highly recommend trying their chicken inasal!

Starbucks

As a tea enthusiast, I appreciate that Starbucks offers a good selection of teas alongside their coffee options. Their matcha lattes are my go-to choice, and they're always made with precision and care. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, and they are happy to accommodate customizations. It's a great place to relax with a cup of tea and enjoy some quiet time.

Subway

Subway offers great value for the price. The footlong subs are generous and can easily be split into two meals. The 'Sub of the Day' promotion is fantastic for getting a delicious sandwich at a discounted price. The quality is consistent across different locations, and the service is usually quick. It's my go-to place for a filling and affordable meal.
